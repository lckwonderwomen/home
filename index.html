Our Story

Women who lead, serve, and lift each other up.

Chartered under Lions Clubs International, the Wonder Women of Kathmandu came together with a single belief — that service is the truest form of leadership.

From eye and health camps to scholarships and disaster relief, our members give their time, skill, and heart to the communities of the Kathmandu Valley. We are mothers, professionals, entrepreneurs and volunteers, united under one motto: We Serve.

  • Compassion in action
  • Sisterhood & mentorship
  • Local roots, global network
  • Transparent impact
A Proud Milestone · LY 2021–2022

Our Charter Club Celebration

The day the Lions Club of Kathmandu — Wonder Women received its official charter was a moment of pride for every member: a sisterhood formally welcomed into the global Lions family.

Surrounded by dignitaries and our sponsoring club, we celebrated in traditional Nepali attire — and were honoured to be recognised as a Model Club by Lions Clubs International Foundation. It marked the beginning of our journey to serve.
